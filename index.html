ABOUT THE GAME

Alien Space Bastards throws you straight into relentless arcade combat.

You pilot your star fighter against procedurally generated waves of alien enemies. No two playthroughs are ever the same. Enemy patterns shift. Swarm behavior adapts. Every run demands focus, reflexes, and smart weapon usage.

At first, the pace feels manageable. You dodge. You shoot. You rack up points.

Then the screen fills with lasers, explosions, and aggressive alien scum pushing you to your limits.

It's basically arcade survival cranked up to eleven.

FAQ

  • Is every playthrough different?
    Yes. The game uses procedural enemy generation, meaning waves are dynamically created. This ensures that no two runs are the same. You’ll need to adapt your strategy each time you play.
  • Is the game beginner-friendly?
    The controls are simple and easy to learn, making it accessible for new players. However, difficulty increases steadily as you progress. Even experienced players will find later waves challenging.
  • Does the game have online features?
    Yes. Alien Space Bastards includes an online high score leaderboard. You can compete globally and compare your best runs with other players.
  • What type of gameplay can I expect?
    It’s a 3D arcade-style shooter with escalating difficulty. Expect fast-paced action, bullet hell scenarios, and strategic use of power-ups. The deeper you progress, the more intense the experience becomes.
  • Are there different weapons?
    Yes. The game includes multiple collectible power-ups and varied weapons to enhance combat. Choosing when and how to use them strategically is key to survival.
